📝 Technical Details

  • Issue: The inline-block layout with line-height: 22px and vertical-align: top causes subtle 1-2px misalignment between icons and text
  • Root Cause: line-height affects text baseline alignment differently than icon rendering, creating inconsistent vertical positioning
  • Solution: Flexbox with align-items: center ensures all child elements (icons and text) are perfectly centered vertically within the 22px container
  • Platform Impact: The misalignment is most noticeable on Windows 11 and macOS due to font rendering differences
  • Visual Difference: While subtle at 100% zoom, the difference becomes clear when zoomed in or when comparing pixel-perfect alignment
